# Review and moderation

To ensure a safe and compliant ecosystem, Jest applies review and moderation mechanisms to games and platform content. These processes help enforce the:
- Acceptable Use Policy
- Game Development and Publishing Agreement

Developers are responsible for ensuring their content complies with these policies at all times. Jest uses a combination of manual review and automated moderation depending on the content type.

## Game approval

Before a game can be published, it must be submitted for review. Games can be played on Jest.com only when they are both approved and set to Public visibility.

Ongoing updates to live games may be subject to additional review, but these reviews are non-blocking.

## Image approval

Images uploaded to the Image Library are reviewed before they can be used in notifications. Only approved images can be referenced in scheduled notifications.

## Notification moderation

Notifications are not manually pre-approved. Instead, they are automatically scanned for compliance at runtime. If a notification contains non-compliant content, it may be blocked from delivery. Blocked events appear in Events > Moderation in the Developer Console.

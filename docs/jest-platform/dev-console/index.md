# Developer console

The Jest Developer Console is the primary dashboard for building and operating games on the Jest platform. From here, you can create games, publish builds, configure monetization, manage your team, and monitor performance.

## Available sections

The Developer Console is organized around two main areas: Game-level management and Account-level tools.

### Game-level management

Within a specific game, you can access:
- **Games** – Configure game properties, submit for review, and control visibility.
- **Builds** – Upload and activate versions of your game.
- **Products** – Configure in-game purchase products.
- **Images** – Upload and manage images used in notifications.
- **Games > Players** – Look up individual player state for customer support.

These sections are available from the top navigation when viewing a specific game.

### Account-level tools

- **Members** – Invite members, assign roles, and manage access permissions.
- **Sandbox users** – Create sandbox users for testing.

This section is available from the left-hand navigation under User.

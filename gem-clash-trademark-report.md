## 1. Executive Summary

**Recommendation: CONDITIONAL PROCEED with documented risk acceptance, OR consider alternative names.**

The name "Gem Clash" has **moderate conflict risk**. Two existing mobile games use nearly identical names in the same genre (match-3 puzzle). ## 3. Risk Assessment

### Risk Level: **MEDIUM-HIGH**

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|-----------|
| Exact name match exists? | **YES** — "Gem Clash" APK exists |
| Same genre? | **YES** — both are match-3 puzzle games |
| Near-identical name exists? | **YES** — "Gems Clash" by Creative Vision Apps |
| Federal trademark registered? | **Likely NO** (not found in search, but TESS should be checked) |
| Same distribution platform? | **NO** — existing apps are Android APK/third-party; ours is Jest web platform |
| Same target audience? | **PARTIALLY** — both target casual mobile gamers, but Jest has a specific SMS/RCS audience |

### Mitigating Factors
1. **Different platform**: Jest is a web-based gaming platform with SMS distribution. The existing "Gem Clash" apps are Android-only APKs distributed via third-party stores (not Google Play directly).
2. **No federal trademark**: Neither appears to have a registered trademark, reducing legal enforcement risk.
3. **Third-party distribution**: The existing apps appear on APK mirror sites (APKPure, Softonic, Uptodown) rather than major app stores, suggesting they are small/indie titles with limited market presence.

### Aggravating Factors
1. **Exact name match**: An app called "Gem Clash" exists in the exact same genre.
2. **Common-law rights**: The existing developers may have common-law trademark rights from actual use.
3. **Consumer confusion**: A player searching for "Gem Clash" could reasonably confuse the products.
